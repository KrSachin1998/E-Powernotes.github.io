About us

Problem related to studies in college life, not getting proper notes, unable to find previous question papers of internal and external examinations then don't worry we have a solution of this . E-Powernotes make the study easier for college students, it provide a platform to share there notes, assignments and previous question paper so that other user can access it, even teachers can share their notes also so that every students can access them sitting at different places.

Users can also like,dislike notes so that other will get idea about the quality of that notes. even user can add to favourite some notes which he/she can find useful. Depending upon the performance of users they will get rank on the leaderboard and the top 3 user of leaderboard will receive a gift and certificate of appreciation from E-Powernotes on the end of each semester.
